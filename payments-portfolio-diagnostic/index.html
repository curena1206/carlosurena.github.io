About Tool Results

Evaluate a commercial payments franchise in minutes.

This lightweight diagnostic scores a payments portfolio across growth, monetization, margin durability, rail readiness, balance contribution, and governance maturity — then produces executive priorities for the next 90 days.

V1 scope
Designed for commercial banking payments portfolios (ACH, wires, RTP/FedNow, cross-border, FX-enabled flows, liquidity overlays).
Portable
The framework is portable to networks & B2B fintechs by swapping rail economics and governance assumptions. Tokenized rails (stablecoins) are a planned V2 extension.
No data is stored. This runs locally in your browser.
What you’ll get
  • Portfolio maturity score (0–100)
  • Monetization / margin durability / readiness sub-scores
  • Pillar heatmap (green / yellow / red)
  • Primary constraints & risks (executive bullets)
  • 90-day priorities with owners and metrics
Tip: Use this in interviews to walk through how you diagnose a franchise and set priorities.
